Our Story

From garage startup to global innovation leader

2020

The Beginning

Founded by a team of engineers and designers who were frustrated with the complexity of existing 3D printers. We started in a small garage with a simple mission: make 3D printing work out of the box.

2021

First Prototype

Developed our first working prototype that combined speed, reliability, and ease of use. This became the foundation for our Core-XY motion system.

2022

Global Launch

Successfully launched our first product line through Kickstarter, becoming one of the most funded 3D printing campaigns in history.

2023

Innovation Leader

Introduced AI-powered print monitoring and multi-material printing, setting new standards for the industry.

Our Mission

To democratize 3D printing by making it accessible, reliable, and enjoyable for creators, engineers, and hobbyists worldwide.

Our Vision

A world where anyone can turn their ideas into reality with the push of a button, unleashing creativity and innovation across all industries.

Our Values
